# MetaDAO ICO Submission - Shenbury Ltd.

## Project Information

- **Project Name:** Shenbury Ltd.
- **Token Name:** Shenbury Provenance Token
- **Token Ticker:** SHBY
- **Description:** Shenbury is building an AI-Powered Provenance Protocol, an autonomous AI swarm that verifies high-value assets, solving the "oracle problem" for RWA tokenization.

## Tokenomics

- **Total Supply:** 25,800,000 SHBY
- **Distribution:**
  - 10,000,000 SHBY - Public sale participants
  - 2,900,000 SHBY - Liquidity pools
  - 12,900,000 SHBY - Performance package and ecosystem

## Financials

- **Minimum Raise Amount:** $750,000
- **Monthly Team Budget:** $125,000 (1/6th of minimum raise)

*To ensure adequate development resources while complying with MetaDAO requirements, we're allocating the maximum allowed 1/6th of our raise to monthly development costs. The $125,000 monthly budget will be allocated primarily to core development, with minimal overhead to ensure maximum value for investors.*

## Performance Package Configuration

We choose to pre-allocate 12.9M tokens to a performance package, split into 5 equal tranches:
- 2.58M tokens unlock at 2x ICO price
- 2.58M tokens unlock at 4x ICO price
- 2.58M tokens unlock at 8x ICO price
- 2.58M tokens unlock at 16x ICO price
- 2.58M tokens unlock at 32x ICO price

Minimum unlock time: 18 months from ICO date.

Our launch is built on six foundational, world-class artifacts from our collection, with a combined valuation over $600-700,000,000 USD. These pieces represent clients in our pipeline and form the underlying assets for demonstrating our verification technology.
